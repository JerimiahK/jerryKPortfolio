ABOUT ME

I was born in Denver, Colorado and raised in Highlands Ranch, a suburb south of Denver. I grew up playing travel hockey, and drums, however, I always had a keen interest in technology and loved playing video games. When I was a kid, I used to love using the map building features in the games and got my first experience with teaching myself how to not just build maps, but learn how to do something from the ground up, without any previous experience. From that point on, I have always had a love for the subject of learning itself, and put the value of knowledge above any material items. Figuring things out by practice has always been the ideal method of learning for me. Drums were always a means of expressing myself, as well as giving me the opportunity to learn and figure out things through repetition and practice. It combined the freedom of being an artists, creating the art, and putting me on a path of always learning new things, and understanding the process. That is why it feels almost natural after almost a decade of being a touring, working musician, to make the transition to becoming a software developer. Many of the same core principles apply across the board from music to computer programming, as well as the ability to still be able to create. I have goals to create work that helps people, makes life more convenient, and contribute in meaningful ways, all while still retaining the aesthetics of imagination and creativity.
